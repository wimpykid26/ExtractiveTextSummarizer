 The Cleveland Cavaliers, also known as the Cavs, are an American professional basketball team based in Cleveland, Ohio.
 The team competes in the National Basketball Association (NBA) as a member of the Central Division of the Eastern Conference.
 The team began play in 1970 as an expansion team, along with the Portland Trail Blazers and Buffalo Braves.
